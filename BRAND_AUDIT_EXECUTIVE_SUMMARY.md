### Typography Analysis
**Standard:** Montserrat font family with proper hierarchy

**Hierarchy Observed:**
- H1: 40-48px, bold (Heroes, main titles)
- H2: 32-36px, semibold (Section titles)
- H3: 24-28px, semibold (Subsections)
- Body: 16px, normal weight (Content text)
- Caption: 12-14px, gray (Supporting text)

**Status:** ✅ Perfect hierarchy across all pages

### Button Consistency
**Primary Buttons:** Lime background, white text, hover animation (FIXED ✅)
**Secondary Buttons:** Outline with lime border, hover to filled
**All Buttons:** 300ms smooth transitions, 5px radius

**Status:** ✅ Consistent across all 22 pages (fixed white text visibility)

### Layout Consistency
**Method:** Flexbox for layouts, proper spacing (Tailwind scale)
**Container Width:** Max-width with responsive padding
**Spacing:** py-12, py-24 for sections, gap-4 for components

**Status:** ✅ Consistent and professional

### Footer Consistency
**Implementation:** Single unified Footer component
**Coverage:** 6 pages using identical footer
**Content:** Brand info, navigation, agents, regions, contact, social, legal

**Status:** ✅ 100% unified and consistent

### Accessibility Compliance

- White on black: 21:1 (WCAG AAA)
